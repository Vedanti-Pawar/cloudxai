Service-aware logic

Azure services don't optimize the same way

CloudDIET applies the right optimization logic to each service family.

Cosmos DB

Capacity models, API/data-model fit, replicas, partitions, consistency, and indexing.

App Services

Plan tiers and eligibility, runtime shape, autoscale vs fixed scale, and allocated vs actual use.

Event Hubs / Messaging

SKU fit, partition and throughput design, and scale-out / scale-back elasticity.

Databricks

Idle & zombie compute, local-disk residency, governance gaps, and commit sizing.

FAQ

The quick answers

Which cloud providers does CloudDIET support?
CloudDIET currently supports Microsoft Azure.
How long does it take to get started?
It is a SaaS platform with no agents or deployment. Configure in minutes with a Service Principal; reporting starts quickly, and optimization opportunities surface over hours to days depending on environment size.
What data can CloudDIET access?
Read-only access is limited to billing, resource metrics, and Azure control-plane metadata. There is no data-plane access to customer content such as tables, schemas, queries, or files.

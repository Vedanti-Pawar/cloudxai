Savings categories

Quick wins first. Bigger savings next.

CloudDIET groups opportunities by the customer effort required to turn identified savings into realized savings.

Minimal effort

Mostly administrative or configuration changes · FinOps / cloud-admin skills · ROI in days to 2 weeks.

Moderate effort

Validation, owner alignment, and a managed change window · app & platform teams · ROI in 2 to 6 weeks.

Planning required

Roadmap, architecture, or migration planning · architects & engineering leads · ROI over 1 to 2 quarters.

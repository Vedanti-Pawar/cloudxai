Why Azure optimization is hard — and what most tools miss

The challenge

The Azure cost optimization challenge

  • Complex pricing. Every Azure service has its own billing model, tiers, SKUs, and licensing.
  • Fragmented opportunities. Waste sits across infrastructure, networking, analytics, and platform services.
  • Workloads keep changing. Usage scales up, down, on, and off — yesterday's answer ages fast.
  • Deep expertise is scarce. Teams need service-by-service Azure engineering context to optimize well.
The baseline

What most FinOps tools focus on

  • Dashboards & reports. Good for spend visibility, tagging, and trend tracking.
  • Basic discount levers. Often focus on RI, Savings Plan, or anomaly nudges.
  • Financial views first. Usually show cost patterns, not root-cause engineering detail.
  • Limited technical depth. They often miss configuration-driven waste across Azure services.
The difference

Why CloudDIET is different

  • Profiles billing, usage & configuration. Analyzed together to uncover the true drivers of waste.
  • Scans hundreds of opportunities. Across IaaS, PaaS, networking, analytics, and platform services.
  • Finds what dashboards miss. Misconfigurations, orphaned resources, underutilization, and SKU/design inefficiencies.
  • Prioritized guidance. Including effort, risk, and estimated savings.
  • Low operational risk. Daily, read-only profiling — without changing resources or touching customer data.

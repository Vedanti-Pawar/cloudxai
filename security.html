Security & data protection

Read-only by design

How CloudDIET accesses, protects, and lets customers control Azure metadata. No agents, no deployment, and no data-plane access to your tables, schemas, queries, or files.

01

What CloudDIET can access

  • Billing, reservation, and savings-plan data.
  • Azure control-plane metadata and Azure Monitor metrics.
  • No data-plane access to tables, schemas, blob contents, queries, or files.
02

Access is read-only and scoped

  • Customer-managed service principal with standard Azure RBAC.
  • Scoped only to selected Azure subscriptions.
  • Read-only control-plane access with no write or modification permissions.
03

How data is protected

  • AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Minimal metadata retained for reporting, with customer-directed purge.
  • Just-in-time, logged employee access limited to a small support group.
04

Customer control and auditability

  • Audit sign-ins and RBAC assignments in Microsoft Entra / Azure.
  • Revoke access anytime by removing credentials, disabling the service principal, or removing RBAC roles.
  • CloudDIET employees cannot use customer service-principal credentials.
